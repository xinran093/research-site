Projects / Research Areas

From an ecological perspective on learning, our research program recognizes the rich, dynamic, and interconnected system where knowledge emerges from interactions between students, knowledge artifacts, digital technologies, as well as their interdependencies.

We focus on designing and investigating the integration of digital technologies within collaborative learning environments, with the goal of supporting productive interactions and knowledge creation in authentic classroom settings.
